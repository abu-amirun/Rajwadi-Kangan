Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for brides and boutique partners alike.

Do you offer wholesale or reseller pricing?

Yes — Rajwadi Kangan offers reseller-friendly wholesale pricing pan-India. Fill in the "Join Our Network" form above or message us on WhatsApp with your store details for current rates and minimum order quantities.

Is Cash on Delivery (COD) available?

Yes, Cash on Delivery is available on eligible orders across India.

Are your pieces safe for sensitive skin?

All Rajwadi Kangan pieces are nickel-free and finished to be gentle on sensitive skin.

Do you take bulk bridal orders?

Yes — we regularly fulfil bulk bridal and wedding-season orders. Reach out early on WhatsApp so we can plan production time around your date.

How can I see the full catalogue?

Use the "View Catalogue" or "Download PDF" buttons in the Catalogue section above, or message us on WhatsApp to request the latest photo pack.
